            No.1 Events Management

            Sunrisers Event Planners, established in 2012, is a team of highly experienced and passionate professionals dedicated to delivering exceptional results in the fields of event management, advertising, marketing, brand promotions, and exhibitions.

            With our creative vision and strong execution capabilities, we provide innovative solutions that help our clients elevate their brands and create unforgettable experiences. Over the years, Sunrisers Event Planners has successfully organized more than 100+ events, earning the trust and satisfaction of numerous happy clients.

            Our talented team of creative thinkers, planners, and event specialists are committed to delivering events that are memorable, impactful, and flawlessly executed. From conceptualization to completion, every project is handled with the highest level of professionalism, quality, and attention to detail.

            At Sunrisers Event Planners, we believe every event should tell a story. Our designers transform venues into spectacular experiences, while our production team ensures that every element is perfectly aligned with the client’s vision. Whether the event is local or international, we work closely with our clients and partners to create events that leave lasting impressions.

            With all expertise under one roof, we ensure seamless planning, execution, and management of events of any size or scale.

            Historic International Women’s Day Celebration by Sunrisers Event Planners

            09-03-2026

            Sunrisers Event Planners proudly celebrated International Women’s Day on 7th March 2023 in a truly historic and unique way.

            The event was conducted inside the running VANDE BHARAT EXPRESS train from Bangalore to Mysore, creating a remarkable milestone. During this special journey, inspiring women, referred to as the “Durga Devis of our Nation,” were honored and celebrated for their achievements and contributions to society.

            This extraordinary initiative created history for the first time in India, as International Women’s Day was celebrated inside a running train with distinguished women achievers.

            The event received wide appreciation and recognition and was officially acknowledged by the prestigious Bharat Book of Records, marking it as a proud moment for Sunrisers Event Planners.
